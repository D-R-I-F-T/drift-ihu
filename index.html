About Us

DRIFT-IHU was formed in first 2025 at International Hellenic University - Alexandria University City. Not much time has passed since then but we already begun to set out on a long journey. This journey,our journey is driven by curiosity, collaboration, and the desire to create a high-performance race car.

From designing and testing to competing in international events, we focus on turning ideas into action and challenges into achievements.
