About Us

DRIFT-IHU was formed in first 2025 at International Hellenic University - Alexandria University City. Not much time has passed since then but we already begun to set out on a long journey. This journey,our journey is driven by curiosity, collaboration, and the desire to create a high-performance race car.

From designing and testing to competing in international events, we focus on turning ideas into action and challenges into achievements.

Mechanical Engineering Students

Our talented mechanical engineers design and build the backbone of our race cars, from chassis to suspension.

Electrical Engineering Students

They handle everything electric: wiring, sensors, control systems, and power management.

Software Engineering Students

Our coders develop the software that powers our autonomous systems and ensure the safety and the performance of our vehicle.

Marketing Team

The marketing team spreads our story, connects with sponsors, and grows our brand worldwide.

Social Media Managers

They manage our online presence, create content, and keep fans updated on our journey.
